# Support & Help

We are here to help you with any questions or issues you may have while using Boo Baby.

## Contact Us

If you need assistance, have feedback, or want to report a problem, please reach out to our support team.

**Email:** [help.dev@boo-baby.com](mailto:help.dev@boo-baby.com)

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my data safe?
Yes, your data is stored securely and encrypted. We prioritize your privacy and do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

### How do I change my baby's profile?
You can update your baby's name, birth date, and other details in the "Baby" section of the app.
