# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **boo-baby** ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect information provided through our authentication provider (Supabase), which may include:
- Email address
- Name (if provided)

### 1.2 App Data (Baby Activity Logs)
To provide the core functionality of the App, we collect and store data related to your baby's activities, including:
- **Feeding Logs**: Type (breast, bottle), amount, duration, and side.
- **Sleep Sessions**: Start and end times.
- **Diaper Changes**: Type (wet, dirty, both), color, and texture.
- **Medications**: Name, dose, and time taken.
- **Pumping Logs**: Amount and side.
- **Baby Profiles**: Name and birth date.

### 1.3 Family Sharing Data
If you use the Family Sharing feature, we store connections between your account and other users you invite to your family group.

### 1.4 Purchase and Device Information
If you choose to purchase a subscription or remove ads, we collect information related to your purchase history and device identifiers through our payment and analytics providers (RevenueCat and Google).


## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
- Provide and maintain the App's features.
- Sync data across your devices and with your family members.
- Generate reports and summaries (e.g., PDF exports).
- Authenticate your access to the App.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Display non-personalized or personalized advertisements (depending on your consent).


## 3. Data Storage and Security
All data is stored securely in the cloud using **Supabase** (PostgreSQL database and Auth). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but please be aware that no storage method is 100% secure.

## 4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal information or baby activity data to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:
- **With Family Members**: Data you record is visible to other members of your family group if you have set up Family Sharing.
- **Service Providers**: We use third-party services for various functionalities:
    - **Supabase**: Database management and authentication.
    - **RevenueCat**: Management of in-app purchases and subscriptions.
    - **Google AdMob**: Serving advertisements.
    These providers have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.


## 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion
You have the right to access, update, or delete your information. 
- You can edit baby logs and profiles directly within the App.
- You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@dsaglam94.com. Upon deletion, all your personal data and associated baby activity logs will be removed from our servers.

## 8. Advertising
We may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve these ads, Google may collect and use certain data, such as your device's advertising ID. You can manage your ad preferences through your device settings.

## 9. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions
Our App uses RevenueCat to processing in-app purchases and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat collects purchase history and device identifiers to ensure your subscription is active across your devices.

## 10. Children's Privacy
While the App tracks data about infants, it is intended for use by parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

## 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
